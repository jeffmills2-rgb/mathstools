Worksheet details

Task = one number line (0%–100%) plus 6 percentage questions.

Totals (100%)

Questions 1–3 will be random totals that are multiples of 10 from 10 to 120 (inclusive).
Seed lets you reproduce the same worksheet later (e.g., “class7A”).

Percent questions

Tick the pool of percentages you want available. Each question will randomly choose 6 (no repeats within a question). If you tick fewer than 6, it will pad using the PDF-style set.
If the pool is empty, or needs padding, PDF-style uses 85% for totals ≤100 and 95% for totals >100.
