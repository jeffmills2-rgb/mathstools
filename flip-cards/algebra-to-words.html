Teacher notes

Levels

Basic uses one-step worded expressions. Expected uses multi-step expressions, grouping, halves, and “difference between” language.

  • Basic: 5 more than x → x + 5
  • Basic: half of a → a ÷ 2
  • Expected: 3 more than twice n → 2n + 3
  • Expected: the sum of y and 7, multiplied by 2 → 2(y + 7)
Suggested routine

Ask students to say the expression aloud before flipping. Encourage multiple equivalent forms where appropriate, such as x ÷ 2 and x/2.
