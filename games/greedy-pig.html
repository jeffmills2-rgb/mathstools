Greedy Pig rules

Students start each round standing. The teacher chooses either a 6-sided or 10-sided die when creating the room, then rolls the die. If a non-poison number is rolled, it is added to the round total. If 2 is rolled as the first roll of a round, it is safe and adds 2 to the round total. After the first roll, 2 is the poison number: students still standing score 0 for that round. Students may press Bank to safely add the current round total to their score. After the chosen number of rounds, the highest total wins.
