Rules

Rows and Columns are fixed for the whole game. The Rows player can only take a card from the marker’s row. The Columns player can only take a card from the marker’s column.

Take the card, add its value to your total, and move the marker to that card’s square. If it is your turn and your active row or column has no cards left, the game ends. Highest total wins.
