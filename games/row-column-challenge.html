Rules

Rows and Columns are fixed for the whole game. The Rows player can only take a number from the marker’s row. The Columns player can only take a number from the marker’s column.

The black arrow marker shows which direction is active: ↔ means a Rows turn, and ↕ means a Columns turn.

Take the number, add its value to your total, and move the marker to that number’s square. If it is your turn and your active row or column has no numbers left, the game ends. Highest total wins.
