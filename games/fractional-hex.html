Rules

How to win

Player 1 wins by making an unbroken chain from the left side to the right side.

Player 2 wins by making an unbroken chain from the top to the bottom.

How to play

On your turn, choose any open hex. A pop-up asks you to simplify the fraction. A correct answer claims the hex for your colour.

Hexes connect through their six sides. Building a blocking wall is also building a possible winning chain, so there are no stalemates.

Mistake modes

Practice Mode: mistakes shake the pop-up and you try again.

Pressure Mode: in Vs Computer, a mistake loses your turn and the computer moves.

Strategy tip

You can claim any open hex. Think about creating your own path while interrupting your opponent’s path.
